About Lap Up Entertainment

10+ Years Experience

Mumbai's Premier Event Management Company

Founded in 2013 by Suraj Mishra, Lap Up Entertainment has established itself as a leading event management company in Mumbai, specializing in creating memorable and impactful experiences.

We pride ourselves on our creative approach, technical expertise, and attention to detail. From small corporate gatherings to large-scale public events, we handle every aspect of event planning and execution with precision and flair.

"Our mission is to transform ideas into extraordinary experiences that leave a lasting impression."
- Suraj Mishra, Founder
